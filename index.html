மருகு (Marugu) - Marjoram (Origanum majorana)

Marugu, also known as Marjoram (Origanum majorana), is a fragrant herb widely used in cooking and traditional medicine. It's a relative of oregano and adds a sweet, floral aroma to dishes.

Key Facts

  • Soil Moisture: Well-drained soil, moderate moisture (avoid waterlogging).
  • Ideal Temperature: 15°C to 25°C (59°F to 77°F).
  • Sunlight: Requires full sunlight for optimal growth.
  • Uses: Commonly used in soups, salads, and herbal teas. Also valued for its medicinal properties.
  • Harvesting: Leaves can be harvested anytime during the growing season, preferably in the morning.
